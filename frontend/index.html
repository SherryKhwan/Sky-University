About Sky University

Sky University, established in 1984 in Karachi, is now a chain of universities with 12 campuses in Karachi, and others located in Hyderabad and Multan as well. This is our 34th year of Academic Excellence. We are affiliated with University of Cambridge, London and Board of Higher Education too. Sky University is also running its own Academy of Teachers’ Training, under the shadow of International Montessori council, USA. Most of our students opt to take the international GCE O and AS/A Level examinations organized by the CIE of UCLES. We have approximately 6000+ students studying in Sky University. Also, more than 3000 families are registered on our social networking pages and website. Sky University (abbreviated as SU) is one of the largest private English medium university systems in Pakistan established in 1984. It offers undergraduate, masters education, and Phd education and preparation for local HSC and the international GCE. SU has earned its outstanding reputation through its students’ successes, its rigorous educational programs and its excellent and dedicated staff. It also promotes and encourages co-curricular activities in all of its campuses.

Our Vision

To impart standardized quality of education, while maintaining its affordability to the masses, in order to empower future generations and settle them in international mainstream.

Our Mission

To establish excellent scholastic standards, hence enabling our students, community and ourselves to become ambassadors of Islam in the light of Quran and Sunnah.
